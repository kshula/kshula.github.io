About Me



Passionate mathematician, economist, and seasoned software developer with a proven track record in research, consulting, and policy analysis.

I am dedicated to creating efficient and innovative solutions that address real-world challenges.

Leveraging a decade-long expertise in statistical analysis, forecasting and modeling, I have seamlessly transitioned into the realm of software programming, specializing in Machine Learning (ML) applications, web development, APIs, Cloud and database management.

Throughout my career, I have successfully led cutting-edge, evidence-based advocacy initiatives and implemented impactful policy analyses. My ability to build consensus among high-level stakeholders, including government officials, private sector leaders, and civil society organizations sets me apart. A skilled communicator, I excel in translating complex economic concepts into easily understandable language for diverse audiences.

I am enthusiastic about continuous learning and am committed to staying at the forefront of technological advancements in software development. My journey from economist to software programmer reflects my eagerness to adapt, grow, and contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
